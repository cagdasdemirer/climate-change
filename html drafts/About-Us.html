Our Aim

G​lobal warming is one of the biggest and  and most important problem of our age.  Our biggest goal is to show our users how important and dramatic consequences this problem has, which many people ignore. By showing them these consequences  we aim to raise their awareness.  It is also our duty to show the users that individual solutions exist for every sector and to explain what can be done to improve the situation.
